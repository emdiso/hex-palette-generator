what is hex?

Hex is a digital designer platform, here to cater to your color palette needs.
If you really enjoy the vibe of a photo and want to capture that in your website,
then what you need is a hex color generator.
Just simply click create, upload the vibey photo, and then save your color palette.
Or you can browse the home page for other vibey photos if you haven't found one yet.
Click on any palette you like, and the css code for the colors will be copied to your clipboard!
:) Happy color stealing :)
